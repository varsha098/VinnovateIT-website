About VIT

Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore is one of the most revered and distinguished engineering institution which holds the rare distinction of being one of the few colleges to impart world-class education along with encouragement towards co-curricular activities which help in overall development and transform students into responsible adults with a strong ethical core. Vellore Institute of Technology was the birth-child of the tireless and relentless perseverance of our respected Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, whose objective since the very inception of this institution has been to provide an environment that would help foster intellectual aptitude, enhance human perception and create a society of extra-ordinary professionals capable of thinking new, out-of-the-box ideas and having the skills to implement them, in order to help the entire society as a whole.




About VinnovateIT

VinnovateIT is an innovation and incubation lab in VIT University, Vellore With the belief of innovation, create, explore and learn. We focus on innovations and creations. We think and give vision to our ideas. Our lab is equipped with every facility a innovator requires to give shape to his/her Innovations. VinnovateIT has been working on projects related to
• Internet of Things.
• Web development.
• Cyber security.
• Data Analytics.
• Mobile App development.
• Creative Design.
VinnovateIT creates a large number of projects based on these topics, we believe in working hard with determination and the greatest strength comes when we work together, thus our strength lies in unity. VinnovateIT creates its own technology, and thus we believe in
